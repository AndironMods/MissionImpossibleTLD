# Mission Impossible – Survival Quest System for The Long Dark

## What Is This Mod?

**Mission Impossible adds a quest system to The Long Dark** that gives you daily, weekly, and monthly goals to work towards. Think of it like a survival checklist that rewards you for accomplishing objectives.

Instead of just wandering and surviving aimlessly, you now have a reason to search for specific items - and you get rewards for doing it!


## How It Works

### Every Day at 9 AM, You Get New Quests:

1. **Daily Quest** - "Collect 6 sticks before tomorrow 9 AM"
2. **Weekly Quest** - "Collect 8 scrap metal by next week"  
3. **Monthly Quest** - "Gather 16 pieces of cloth this month"

You accomplish these by simply playing normally - pick up items you find, and the mod automatically tracks your progress.
When each period ends (tomorrow, next week, next month), you automatically receive a reward - maybe food, ammunition, clothes, or tools.

## Main Features

### Daily Quests
- New quest every morning at 9 AM
- Due by next morning at 9 AM (24 hours)
- Easier tasks, quicker rewards
- Great for steady progression

### Weekly Quests  
- New quest every Monday at 9 AM
- Due in 7 days
- Harder challenges
- Bigger rewards

### Monthly Quests
- New quest at the start of each month
- Due in 30 days
- Toughest challenges
- Best rewards

### How Rewards Work
- Complete a quest by the deadline? Get a reward!
- Didn't finish? No punishment - a new quest just generates
- Rewards scale to your difficulty (easier difficulties = more generous rewards)

### Easy Customization
- **Choose how many quests you want** - Want 3 daily quests instead of 1? Easy!
- **Adjust difficulty scaling** - Make quests easier or harder
- **Toggle quest types** - Only want daily quests? Turn off weekly/monthly
- **Show/hide reward details** - Keep it a surprise or see what you're working for


## Getting Started

### Installation

1. **Download MelonLoader** - Required foundation for mods  
   Visit: https://github.com/LavaGang/MelonLoader/releases

2. **Download ModSettings** - Lets you configure the mod in-game  
   Visit: https://github.com/DigitalzombieTLD/ModSettings/

3. **Download MissionImpossibleTLD Mod**  
   Get it from the Releases section of this mod

4. **Extract into your Mods folder:**
   ```
   C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\TheLongDark\Mods\
   ```
   Your folder should look like this:
   - MissionImpossible.dll
   - GearLookup.json

5. **Launch game** - Mod loads automatically  


## Customization (In-Game Menu)

Once installed, open **Options → Mod Settings → Mission Impossible** to:

- **Toggle quests ON/OFF** - Want only daily quests? Turn off weekly/monthly
- **Change quest count** - Increase/decrease how many active quests you have
- **Adjust difficulty** - Make quests more or less demanding
- **Hide rewards** - Keep them a surprise!
- **Control logging** - Show/hide progress messages

## FAQ

**Q: What if I don't complete a quest on time?**  
A: No penalty! A new quest simply generates. The incomplete quest is removed.

**Q: Can I see what reward I'll get before completing the quest?**  
A: By default, no - but you can enable it in your Mod Settings!

**Q: Do quests get harder as I progress?**  
A: The mod scales based on game difficulty, not your progress. Higher difficulties = harder quests + better rewards.

**Q: Can I turn off specific quest types?**  
A: Yes! Disable daily, weekly, or monthly quests individually in Mod settings.

**Q: Will this mod affect my save?**  
A: No. All quest data is stored separately. You can remove the mod anytime without losing progress.

**Q: What if I want to reset all quests?**  
A: Change any Settings in Mod Setting - new quests generate immediately.


## Tips & Tricks

- **Play normally** - Quests work best when you just play the game. The mod tracks pickups automatically.
- **Difficulty affects quests** - Playing Interloper? Expect harder quests but better rewards. Playing Pilgrim? Easier quests, more forgiving.
- **Check back regularly** - New quests spawn every day/week/month at 9 AM. Check in to see what's next!
- **Stock items** - If you know a weekly quest wants wolf pelts, grab them when you can. Rewards aren't time-sensitive after completion.
- **Always new goals** - Quest system runs infinitely. Every completed quest spawns a new one.
